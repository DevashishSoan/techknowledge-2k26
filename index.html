// About the Fest

What is
TECHKNOWLEDGE?

TECHKNOWLEDGE 2K26 is SSIT Campus's flagship annual tech fest — the most ambitious edition yet. Two days, seven elite competitive disciplines, five hundred minds colliding in the pursuit of excellence.

From algorithmic combat to cybersecurity warfare, from machine learning arenas to rapid UI design sprints — every event is engineered to push your limits and redefine what you think is possible.

Organized by Devashish Soan, TECHKNOWLEDGE 2K26 brings together the sharpest minds from SSIT and 30+ colleges across Maharashtra. The battlefield is set. Your rivals are already preparing.
